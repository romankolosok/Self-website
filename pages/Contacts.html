Where do I live

I live in very lovely dorm, Hammond Hall, on men-only floor. I really like where I live, it's very comfortable and quiet. All my neighbours are very well-behaved, neat and silent people, especially on Friday - Sunday. Since I dont want to be different from the others I also do the same as my neighbours usually do: loudly listening to my crappy music, laughing and loudly speaking at night and sometimes practice to play guitar at midnight.

My roommate has even more "equipment" than I have, he has a humongous pc, with 2 monitors and a bunch of bulky peripherals to it.
